**Voiceover Script:**

### **Slide 4: A Universal Challenge: The Gardener's Dilemma**

**Voiceover Script:**
*"This challenge is perfectly encapsulated in the simple act of caring for a plant. We buy sensors, we set reminders, but the plant is a living thing with changing needs. Is it getting more sun today? Is the air drier? We are still just guessing. This is a microcosm of a larger problem in technology: the gap between data and true, ambient intelligence."*

### **Slide 5: The Vision: Project Lavender**

**Voiceover Script:**
*"My vision was to create a system that didn't just give me data, but took intelligent action. This is a shift from a smart device to a true ambient care system."*
